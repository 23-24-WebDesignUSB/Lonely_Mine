Ngọt

A rather simple start with 10 songs, bringing a new music genre into the play, called Indie Rock. The album was released completely digitally on Soundcloud, Spotify and Youtube, with no physical CD. Marking the start of how they will soon become the most famous Rock band in Vietnam.


The album name is also the band name, both meaning "Sweet", although have no release of physical collectibles for collectors, but can be considered the most iconic album of the band
