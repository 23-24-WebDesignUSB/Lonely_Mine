Salads

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Assorted vegetables (such as tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, carrots, etc.)
  • Sunflower seeds (optional)
  • Dressing of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens and vegetables.
  2. Chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, layer the mixed greens as the base.
  4. Add the assorted vegetables on top of the greens.
  5. Sprinkle a small handful of sunflower seeds for added crunch (optional).
  6. Drizzle the salad with your favorite dressing.
  7. Toss the salad gently to evenly distribute the dressing.
  8. Serve and enjoy your salad!

Feel free to add your own twist to the mix. Its just greens with sauce.
