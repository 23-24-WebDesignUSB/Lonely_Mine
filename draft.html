Ngọt

About

A Indie Rock band from Vietnam, consisting of Vu Dinh Trong Thang (singer), Phan Viet Hoang (bassist), Nguyen Hung Nam Anh (drums), Hoang Chi Trung (guitarist). They started making music in 2016 and have released 4 albums by February 2024.
